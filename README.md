## Test Your Skills!
Try to reach the goal without touching the walls.
How steady is your hand?
Let's find out! Try and beat all four levels!

---
## How to Play?
Use your mouse cursor to lead the cube to the goal. Try to avoid touching the walls or the game will restart.
